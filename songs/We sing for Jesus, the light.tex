\songtitle{We sing for Jesus, the light (litanies)}
\begin{song}

We sing for Jesus, the Light! 
He conquers the darkness, \\
la luce, vince le tenebre. \\
We sing for Jesus, the Light! 
He conquers the darkness, \\
la luce, e Christo Gesu!

(Response: Pray for us! / All the saints, pray for us!) \\
\end{song}
